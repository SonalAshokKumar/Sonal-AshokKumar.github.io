About Me

Hey ! I’m Sonal Ashok Kumar, a former finance turned data detective with a twist of interior design flair. You could say I’ve crunched numbers on spreadsheets and arranged living rooms with equal passion. In finance, I mastered the art of attention to detail (because, you know, numbers don’t like to be wrong), and as an interior designer, I learned that solving a problem is way more fun when creativity’s involved. My data-driven journey began when I realized that finance wasn’t just about money—it was about stories hidden in the data. So, I dove headfirst into a comprehensive data analysis bootcamp and picked up some cool tools along the way: Excel, Python, SQL, Tableau. I’ve flexed these new data muscles on projects such as GameCo, Preparing for Influenza Season, Instacart Basket Analysis, and the Blockbuster Rockbuster Stealth Data Analysis. Now, I’m on the hunt for an analyst role where I can bring my quirky blend of detail-obsessed analysis and design-thinking innovation to help make data-driven decisions that actually make a difference. Ready to turn data into a masterpiece? Let’s talk!
